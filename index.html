About Us

Our Story

Excel United International Co., Ltd. (EUI) is a foreign export-import company headquartered in Bangkok, Thailand, with a branch office in Yangon, Myanmar.

The company specializes in import, export, and distribution of food, beverage, and cosmetic products.

EUI serves as a key player in Myanmar's consumer goods market, providing quality products to wholesalers, retailers, and general trade channels nationwide.
